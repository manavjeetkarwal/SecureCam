What SecureCam does

A complete camera-to-viewer security flow

One device works as the camera and another device works as the viewer. The camera sends live video, location, motion activity, and alert events. The viewer can watch, record, capture photos, open the activity page, and trigger an alarm when needed.

Core Features

Live video streamingWatch the camera in real time with audio and video.
Photo captureSave important frames directly from the live stream.
Video recordingRecord the stream and keep a saved copy for review.
Private chatExchange messages between camera side and viewer side.

Security Features

Motion detectionTrack movement activity over time for analysis.

Activity intelligenceSee motion graph, report table, and risk summary.
Step 1

Start the camera

Open the camera page, start your device camera, and get a camera ID for connection.

Step 2

Join from viewer

Enter the camera ID on the live page to watch the stream and use viewer controls.

Step 3

Photos and videos are stored in dedicated pages so you can download or delete them later.
